## 1) Hook Factory (10-second obsession)
**Input:** a theme + vibe + target emotion  
**Output:** 10 hook angles with 1-line lyrical premise + delivery note + replay trigger.

Example prompt:
- “Give me 12 hook angles for ‘I miss you but I’m better now’ — bittersweet, confident, modern pop.”

## 2) TikTok Loop Concept (30 seconds that replays)
**Input:** concept + audience + filming constraints  
**Output:** 3 TikTok formats: opener line, scene plan, cut points, loop mechanic.

Example prompt:
- “Design a 25–35s loop concept for a bedroom performance video. One prop, one angle.”

## 3) Section Blueprint (structure over vibes)
**Input:** hook + vibe + reference structure preference (optional)  
**Output:** verse/pre/chorus/bridge plan with escalation rules and a “what changes each repeat” map.

Example prompt:
- “Turn this hook into a full structure. Make chorus 2 hit harder without changing the words too much.”

## 4) Genre Flip (same payload, different skin)
**Input:** base idea + source genre + target genre  
**Output:** instrumentation swap, groove notes, melody contour direction, delivery changes, tempo band.

Example prompt:
- “Convert this alt-pop idea into cumbia and then into southern rock blues. Keep the emotional payload.”

## 5) Viral Signal Audit (tighten the idea)
**Input:** draft hook + concept  
**Output:** scorecard: replay value, novelty, clarity, tension, meme-ability + precise fixes.

Example prompt:
- “Audit this concept for replay triggers and simplicity. Give fixes without rewriting the whole thing.”

## 6) Creative Partner Mode (what to test next)
**Input:** current draft + goal (“more emotional”, “more funny”, “more shareable”)  
**Output:** 5 testable edits, 3 A/B variants, and what to watch for in comments/retention.

Example prompt:
- “Give me 3 A/B hook variants and tell me what retention signal each one targets.”

## 7) Content Series Planning
**Input:** artist identity + 7-day posting plan  
**Output:** daily concept prompts, filming formats, and a constraint-based series theme.

Example prompt:
- “Create a 7-day series plan: same chorus, different micro-genre each day.”

## 8) Collab Brief (send to a producer / co-writer)
**Input:** concept + references + constraints  
**Output:** one-page brief: vibe, structure, hook, tempo, do/don’t, success criteria.

Example prompt:
- “Write a collab brief for this idea to send a producer. Keep it one page.”
